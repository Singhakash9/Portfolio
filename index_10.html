About

Hello! 👋 I'm Akash

I'm a CCBA-certified Business Analyst based in Toronto, with 3+ years of experience bridging the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. My work spans healthcare operations at Canadian Blood Services, digital media at Canadian Jeweller Magazine, and strategy analytics at Edureka.

What I actually do: I run process discovery sessions, translate what stakeholders say they want into what they actually need, write requirements that developers can act on, and build dashboards that give leadership visibility they didn't have before.

I hold a Postgraduate Certificate in Information Systems Business Analysis from George Brown College (completed August 2025), and both the CCBA and ECBA from IIBA — the internationally recognized standard for Business Analysis professionals.

When I'm not mapping processes, I'm travelling or reading about business strategy.
